# OffGridFilters.com Affiliate Site Master Plan

> **Note:** This document is **advisory guidance**, not a set of hard rules.
> The strategies and recommendations below should be **considered during decision-making**
> but are not decisive — use your judgment and adapt based on research, data, and context.

---

## Project Goal

Build a US-focused Amazon affiliate authority site targeting off-grid, survival, home, RV, and outdoor water filtration products.

---

## Product Selection Strategy

Do not pick random best sellers. Use a tiered strategy:

- **Tier 1:** High-demand authority products
- **Tier 2:** Mid-competition revenue drivers
- **Tier 3:** Low-competition fast-ranking products

Target price range: **$40–$300**

Prioritize buyer-intent keywords over brand keywords.

---

## Approved Product Categories

- Off-Grid Gravity Filters
- Survival Filters
- Camping & Backpacking Filters
- RV Water Filters
- Whole-House & Well Water Filters
- Countertop Filters
- Replacement Filters

---

## Initial Top-50 Product Target Strategy

- Use Amazon US best seller lists.
- Pick products with **4.3+ rating**, **200+ reviews**, and strong demand.
- Use canonical Amazon links only: `https://www.amazon.com/dp/ASIN`

## Article Content Strategy

Each article should include:

- Buyer intent intro
- Product comparison table
- Pros & cons
- Who it's best for
- Who should avoid
- Cost per liter / lifespan math
- CTA to Amazon

---

## Revenue Optimization

- Build upgrade ladders
- Target replacement filter recurring purchases
- Create price-tier pages (Under $50, $100, $200)
- Interlink pillar → money pages → comparisons
