# Category Research — OffGrid Filters

> Research into each product category: buyer priorities, key comparison criteria, positioning.

---

## 1. Countertop & Pitcher Filters

**What buyers prioritize:**
1. Ease of use (no installation required)
2. Taste improvement
3. Contaminant removal (lead, chlorine)
4. Filter replacement cost/frequency
5. Refrigerator fit and capacity

**Key comparison criteria:**
- Filtration technology (carbon vs RO)
- NSF certifications (42 vs 53 vs 401)
- Filter life (gallons per filter)
- Cost per gallon of filtered water
- Capacity (cups per fill)

**Price ranges:**
- Entry-level pitchers: $20–$35
- Premium dispensers: $30–$45
- Countertop RO systems: $200–$320

**Our strongest products:**
- Amazon Basics pitcher (unbeatable budget)
- Brita UltraMax (family capacity king)
- Bluevua RO100ROPOT-UV (premium no-install RO)

**Search intent mapping:**
- "best water filter pitcher" → category roundup
- "brita vs pur" → comparison page
- "water filter pitcher for lead" → Brita Elite or Bluevua review
- "countertop RO system" → Bluevua reviews

## 2. Under-Sink Filters

**What buyers prioritize:**
1. Contaminant removal breadth (TDS, lead, PFAS)
2. Flow rate / GPD
3. Installation complexity
4. Ongoing filter replacement cost
5. Cabinet space requirements

**Key comparison criteria:**
- GPD (gallons per day) capacity
- Filtration stages
- TDS reduction percentage
- Pure-to-waste ratio (for RO)
- NSF certifications
- Tank vs tankless design

**Price ranges:**
- Budget RO systems: $150–$200 (iSpring)
- Premium tankless RO: $400–$500 (Waterdrop)
- Commercial-grade: $390–$505 (Pentair Everpure)

**Our strongest products:**
- iSpring RCC7AK (best value, most reviewed)
- Waterdrop G3P600 (best tankless, most features)
- Pentair H-1200 (best quality, restaurant-grade)

## 3. Refrigerator Filters

**What buyers prioritize:**
1. Compatibility with their specific fridge model
2. OEM vs aftermarket quality concerns
3. NSF certifications for health contaminants
4. Ease of installation
5. Price per replacement cycle

**Key comparison criteria:**
- Brand compatibility (Whirlpool, GE, Samsung, LG)
- NSF certification level (42 vs 53 vs 401)
- Gallon capacity per filter
- OEM vs aftermarket
- RFID requirements (GE models)
- Cost per 6-month replacement cycle

**Price ranges:**
- Aftermarket bulk: $3–$17 per filter (AQUACREST)
- OEM mid-range: $37–$49 (Samsung, GE)
- OEM premium: $48–$54 (everydrop)

**Our strongest products:**
- GE XWFE (most capable, 50+ contaminants)
- everydrop Filter 1 (best for Whirlpool-family)
- AQUACREST 6-pack (unbeatable budget)

1. Compatibility with their existing pitcher
2. Cost per filter / cost per gallon
3. Filter lifespan
4. Contaminant removal claims
5. Brand trust

**Key comparison criteria:**
- Cost per gallon of filtered water
- Filter life (gallons)
- NSF certifications
- Pack size value

## 5. Survival & Portable Filters

**What buyers prioritize:**
1. Weight and packability
2. Filtration effectiveness (bacteria, protozoa)
3. Ease of use in the field
4. Durability
5. Price per unit

**Key comparison criteria:**
- Micron rating
- Weight per unit
- Capacity (liters per filter life)
- Bacteria removal rate (log reduction)
- Virus removal (filters can't; tablets can)
- Included accessories (bags, syringes)

**Price ranges:**
- Budget straws: $7–$10 per unit (Timain)
- Mid-range straws: $8–$10 per unit (Membrane Solutions, NatureNova)
- Chemical purification: $0.40 per tablet (MSR Aquatabs)

**Critical differentiator:** Filter straws remove bacteria/protozoa but NOT viruses. MSR Aquatabs kill viruses. The optimal survival kit combines both.

## 6. Whole House & Sediment Filters

**What buyers prioritize:**
1. Micron rating effectiveness
2. Housing compatibility
3. Filter lifespan
4. Cost per replacement cycle
5. Flow rate maintenance

**Key comparison criteria:**
- Micron rating (1, 5, 10, 20)
- Filter media (string wound, pleated, spun)
- Standard housing fit (10x2.5" universal)
- Dirt-holding capacity
- Price per filter in multi-packs

**Price ranges:**
- Budget string wound: $4–$6 per filter (Membrane Solutions multi-pack)
- Standard replacement: $30–$35 (Generic single)
- Commercial/specialty: $93+ (Elkay WaterSentry)

**Our strongest product:** Membrane Solutions 6-pack at $4.39/filter — unbeatable value for standard sediment filtration.
